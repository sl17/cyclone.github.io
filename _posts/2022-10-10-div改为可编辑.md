## div改为可编辑


### div改为可编辑

```
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
  <meta charset="UTF-8">
  <title>Document</title>
  <style>
    div{
      width: 200px;
      height: 150px;
      border: 1px solid #ddd;
    }
  </style>
</head>
<body>
  <div contenteditable="true" onInput="enterCont(this)"></div>
  <script>
    function enterCont(e){
      console.log(e.innerText);
    }
  </script>
</body>
</html>
```
